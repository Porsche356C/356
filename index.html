This website was created to assist with the maintenance of a 1964 356C 1600 coupe. There are two parts to be this site.

  1. Nut and Bolt finishes - this information is 98% derived from the factory parts manuals with the adition of common accepted information, for example the finish of brake calipers.
  2. VW replacement parts - Porsche parts are not readily avaiolable in Australia, so I purchase VW parts where they are direct replacement. I may also include the VW parts list from Harry Pellows Secrets of the Inner Circle.

The website is opensource, meaning anyone can congtribute to it. The source files can be found at https://porsche356bc.github.io
